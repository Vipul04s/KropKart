Privacy Policy - KropKart

Last updated: September 7, 2024

Welcome to AgriConnect, your trusted agriculture B2B platform connecting buyers and sellers. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Business information (such as company name, address, and registration details)
  • Personal information of company representatives (name, email address, phone number)
  • Transaction data (including product listings, purchase orders, and communication logs)
  • Usage data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) collected automatically when you use our platform
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience and analyze platform traffic

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers on our platform
  • To verify the legitimacy of businesses using our services
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To send relevant market updates, product recommendations, and promotional offers
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your business and personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users of the platform as necessary to facilitate transactions (e.g., sharing contact details between matched buyers and sellers)
  • With third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., payment processors, logistics partners)
  • In response to legal requests or to comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Your Rights and Choices

As a user of our platform, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your business and personal information
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and sending an email to the primary contact associated with your account.
